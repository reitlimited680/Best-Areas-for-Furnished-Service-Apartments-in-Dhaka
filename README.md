## Best Areas for Furnished Service Apartments in Dhaka

### Bashundhara R/A – The Top Choice for Furnished Service Apartments

Among all residential areas in Dhaka, **Bashundhara Residential Area (R/A)** stands out as one of the most desirable locations for serviced apartment living. Over the years, this neighborhood has developed into a modern, well-planned community that combines convenience with comfort.

One of the biggest advantages of Bashundhara R/A is its organized infrastructure. Wide roads, reliable security, modern buildings, and easy access to major city routes make daily life significantly more comfortable. Residents enjoy proximity to leading educational institutions, hospitals, shopping centers, restaurants, and recreational facilities.

For visitors seeking a [fully furnished serviced apartment](https://www.hotels.com.bd/residential-apartment-service/), Bashundhara R/A offers a wide variety of options. Many apartments feature stylish interiors, spacious bedrooms, equipped kitchens, high-speed internet, housekeeping services, laundry facilities, and 24-hour security. These features create a living experience that feels much closer to home than a traditional hotel room.

Families particularly appreciate the calm atmosphere, while business travelers value the convenient access to commercial districts and transportation networks. The combination of modern amenities and a peaceful residential setting make Bashundhara R/A a preferred destination for both short and extended stays.

### Gulshan – Dhaka’s Premium Business District

**Gulshan** is widely recognized as one of the most prestigious neighborhoods in Dhaka. It serves as a center for international business, diplomatic missions, multinational companies, and luxury living.

Furnished serviced apartments in Gulshan often cater to executives and expatriates who require premium accommodation. Residents enjoy easy access to corporate offices, embassies, upscale restaurants, cafés, and shopping destinations.

Although rental costs tend to be higher than in other parts of the city, many people consider the convenience and prestige worth the investment.

### Banani – Modern Urban Living

Located adjacent to Gulshan, **Banani** offers a lively and energetic environment. The area is known for its excellent dining scene, entertainment venues, commercial offices, and vibrant community atmosphere.

Fully furnished serviced apartments in Banani are especially popular among professionals and short-term business travelers. The neighborhood offers a good balance between work and leisure, making it ideal for individuals who want to stay connected to the city's active lifestyle.

### Dhanmondi – A Family-Friendly Option

**Dhanmondi** has long been one of Dhaka’s most established residential areas. The neighborhood offers a mix of educational institutions, healthcare facilities, shopping centers, and recreational spaces.

Serviced apartments in Dhanmondi attract families, medical travelers, and long-term residents. The presence of renowned hospitals and schools adds to the area’s appeal. Residents enjoy a comfortable lifestyle with easy access to daily necessities and community amenities.

### Uttara – Convenience Near the Airport

For travelers who frequently fly in and out of Dhaka, **Uttara** provides an excellent location. Situated near Hazrat Shahjalal International Airport, the area offers convenient transportation and a growing range of residential and commercial facilities.

Fully furnished serviced apartments in Uttara often feature modern designs and competitive pricing. The neighborhood has become increasingly attractive to families and professionals seeking a quieter environment away from the city center.
